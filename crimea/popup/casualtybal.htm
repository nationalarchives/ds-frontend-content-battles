Casualty return for the period including Balaklava

This is the casualty return for the period that includes the Battle of Balaklava. Casualty figures for the Charge of the Light Brigade total 110 killed, 130 wounded and 58 captured, a loss of some 40% of the brigade's strength.

Catalogue reference: WO 1/369 f. 685 (22-26 Oct 1854)
