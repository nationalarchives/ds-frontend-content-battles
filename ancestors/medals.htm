Medals

Participants in wars and armed conflicts were often entitled to a medal. These could be either for gallantry or for participation in a particular campaign. The National Archives holds details for medals issued up to and including the First World War but very few records later than that date have been released, with the exception of some Second World War army gallantry medals.

The Ministry of Defence may in appropriate circumstances issue or reissue medals awarded after 1920. Medals awarded earlier than that date will not be reissued. Contact addresses are given in the relevant guides referred to below.
